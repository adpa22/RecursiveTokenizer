The Future of Technology and Us

Step into a city and what do you see? Most likely people crowding the streets and carrying on with their lives. But soon it won’t just be people, there will be robots walking around,interacting with us, asking us how our day was, opening doors for us, and even performing surgeries on us. This may seem unusual to some people, but it may actually become normal in the future. Technology has become so ingrained into society that it has become a regular part of people’s lives.
